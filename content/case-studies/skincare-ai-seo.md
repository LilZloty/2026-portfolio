## The Challenge

A fast-growing skincare DTC brand was struggling to scale their SEO efforts. With 200+ products and weekly new launches, manually optimizing every product page was eating up 50+ hours per month of their team's time.

**The Pain Points:**

- Manual product descriptions taking 20+ minutes each
- Inconsistent SEO quality across the catalog
- No time for collection page optimization
- Missing schema markup on most pages
- Slow organic traffic growth despite quality products

## The Solution

I built them a custom AI-powered SEO system that handles the heavy lifting while maintaining brand voice and quality.

### The AI SEO Pipeline

**1. Bulk Meta Optimization**

- Custom prompts trained on their brand voice
- Automatic title tag generation (60 char optimized)
- Compelling meta descriptions with CTAs
- Keyword integration based on search volume data

**2. Product Description System**

- AI generates first draft based on product attributes
- Maintains consistent brand tone
- Includes benefit-focused copy
- Structured for readability (headers, bullets, etc.)

**3. Schema Markup Automation**

- Product schema with ratings and pricing
- FAQ schema for common questions
- Organization and breadcrumb schema
- Automatic updates when products change

### Speed Optimization (Bonus)

While implementing the SEO system, I also tackled their speed issues:

- PageSpeed improved from 52 to 89
- Load time reduced by 58%
- Core Web Vitals all in the green
